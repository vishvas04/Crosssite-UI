Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Telangana Complaint Portal?

    Telangana Complaint Portal is a portal which can be used by the people residing in telangana to lodge their complaints regarding any problem. Then the problem will be directly taken to the notice of respective higher authorities to lookup and Solve.

  • What other information is provided in Telangana Complaint Portal(TCP)?

    In TCP the users can also get the information regarding the constitutional memebers of Telangana.

  • What happens when the complaint is not resolved by the respective higher authorities?

    In most cases if the proof is strong, the problem will be resolved as soon as possible. If the proof is not strong, then there will be more chances that the problem lodged will not be resolved. So when lodging a complaint make sure to have a strong proof in hand.

  • What kind of proof can be submitted?

    The proof can be of any kind like video,photos,audio and etc...

  • What happens if wrong proof is submitted?

    The proof submitted will be checked first and in that process if the submitted proof is found to be illegal like morphed audio,video,photos then the user is liable for punishment and for penalty upto 1 lakh for wasting the time of the authorities and to submit illegal proofs to government.
